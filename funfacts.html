The plural form of moose is moose.

Moose can kick in all directions, not just backwards.

Moose are the largest of all deer species.

Moose are so tall that eating shrubs that are low to the ground can be difficult.

They can run up to 35 miles per hour, and can trot at 20 miles per hour.

The largest span for moose antlers recorded was 83 inches (210 centimeters).

A full grown moose has few natural predators besides the siberian tiger.

Moose can eat up to 73 pounds of food a day in the summer, and 34 in the winter.

A moose can swim up to 6 miles per hour.

They have 32 teeth, and big mouths apparently.
